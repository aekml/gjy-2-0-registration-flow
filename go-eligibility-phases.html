    D1 -- No --> L1([Legacy / prior-rule bucket<br/>or Not Eligible as per policy]):::legacy

    G1 -- Yes --> G1A{Plot <= 1200<br/>and Floors in GO-1 set?}:::decision

    A([Flag outcomes]):::start --> B{Scenario type}:::decision
    B -- Unserviced + GO-1 criteria --> E1([Eligible under G.O-1]):::eligible

    B -- Serviced + GO-2 criteria after 31.05.2026 --> F2([G.O-2]):::approve
    B -- Tariff not LT-1 / invalid registration / failed criteria --> Z1([Not Eligible]):::reject
    B -- Service date <= 04.04.2025 --> L1([Legacy / prior-rule bucket]):::legacy
